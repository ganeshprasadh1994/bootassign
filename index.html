About Sarathy Bikes Inc. A Multi branded Bike Showroom

Equipped with sales and marketing team cater to the needs of people in and around Chennai, Coimbatore and Bengaluru with the best matched models of Bikes. We offer best services to our customers to complete satisfaction. BIKERS CLUB

We are dedicated to positively promoting all aspects of motorcycling with an emphasis on Safety and Rider awareness. At Sarathy, we do not care what you choose to ride, only that you love to ride! Sarathy Bikers Club is open to all brands/types of motorcycles (150 CC and above). The club welcomes both beginners as well as experienced riders, we have a corner marking rule so no one gets lost. Information on all rides and other upcoming club activities are posted to members and updated in the site. We ride together as much as we can, join others to support their events and organize fund raising efforts to help out those in our community who are in need.
